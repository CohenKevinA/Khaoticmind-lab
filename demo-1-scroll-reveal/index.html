demo 1 // staggered fade-in on scroll

Block One

This card fades and slides into view once it enters the viewport. Nothing fancy under the hood — just opacity and a Y-axis transform, animated by GSAP.

Block Two

Each card is triggered independently by ScrollTrigger, so they animate as you scroll past them rather than all at once on page load.

Block Three

This is the exact mechanic that'll drive family reunion timeline entries or photo gallery items appearing one after another as visitors scroll.

Block Four

Once this pattern feels right, we tune timing, easing, and distance to match the final site's personality before porting it over.
